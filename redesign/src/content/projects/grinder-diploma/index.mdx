description: |
  For my diploma project, I undertook the design of a coffee grinder. I carefully recreated an exact 3D model based on the drawings and photographs of a KitchenAid coffee grinder. Using Solidworks, I crafted both the exterior casing and all the internal components of the coffee grinder. The final visualization was rendered in Blender 3D, showcasing the detailed and accurate representation of the product.
