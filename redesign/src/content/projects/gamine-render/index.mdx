  Gamine New York is a fashion-forward eyewear brand that combines innovative design with meticulous craftsmanship, inspired by the dynamic energy of New York City. Led by Sergei Zubkouski, the brand empowers individuals to express their unique style with timeless and elegant eyewear.

  I began collaborating with Gamine NYC in the fall of 2022 as a 3D modeling and visualization specialist. During our productive partnership, I created numerous 3D models of eyewear based on drawings provided by the company, as well as countless renders of various frame and lens combinations.

  Over time, my responsibilities expanded to include creating animations and preparing 3D models for a platform that allows users to virtually try on glasses in real-time.
